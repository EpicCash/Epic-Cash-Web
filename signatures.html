Brick Abode has GPG-signed the Epic binaries.

1. Getting our key

Our PGP key is at the bottom of this file. However, you can also find it on the public key servers, either by id:

$ gpg --receive-keys E8C092AC9674D578FCA90511EE3CA062DFE1B3FC

or by address:

$ gpg -k epiccash@brickabode.com
pub   rsa3072 2019-08-31 [SC]
      E8C092AC9674D578FCA90511EE3CA062DFE1B3FC
uid           [ unknown] Epic Cash <epiccash@brickabode.com>
sub   rsa3072 2019-08-31 [E]

2. Verifying our packages

Once you’ve imported our key, you can check the signatures on our binary:

$ gpg --verify epic.zip.sig epic.zip
gpg: Signature made Mon Sep  2 19:24:34 2019 -03
gpg:                using RSA key E8C092AC9674D578FCA90511EE3CA062DFE1B3FC
gpg: Good signature from "Epic Cash <epiccash@brickabode.com>" [unknown]
gpg: WARNING: This key is not certified with a trusted signature!
gpg:          There is no indication that the signature belongs to the owner.
Primary key fingerprint: E8C0 92AC 9674 D578 FCA9  0511 EE3C A062 DFE1 B3FC

Do likewise for all Epic binaries.
